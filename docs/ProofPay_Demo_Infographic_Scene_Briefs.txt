PROOFPAY — HEDERA x402 BOUNTY DEMO
Infographic scene descriptions and generation briefs
Updated: 2026-07-21

PURPOSE

This file defines one self-contained infographic for each of the eleven
ProofPay demo scenes. Each brief explains the story, information hierarchy,
diagram type, visual composition, exact deterministic overlay content, and an
image-generation prompt.

Use these briefs with:

- docs/ProofPay_Demo_Transcript.txt for narration and exact on-screen claims;
- docs/ProofPay_Demo_Visualization_Prompts.txt for the shared cinematic style,
  negative prompt, branding, provenance, and production checklist; and
- docs/evidence/proof-bundle-2026-07-21.json for public performed-payment facts.

EVIDENCE BOUNDARY

These are explanatory infographics for an already-completed Hedera testnet
payment. They must not suggest that another payment executes during playback.
HashScan and the Hedera mirror node prove ledger settlement. The ProofPay
receipt independently binds that settlement to the request, evidence, output,
source date, and software version.

GLOBAL INFOGRAPHIC RULES

Canvas:
- 16:9 landscape, preferably 1920 × 1080 or 1536 × 1024.
- Dark near-black background with graphite panels.
- Hedera green for consensus and verification.
- Warm gold for transferred value and confirmed payment evidence.
- DeltaSignal red only for brand accents and the signed receipt seal.
- Cool blue for facilitator-sponsored network fees.

Typography:
- Do not generate exact labels, account IDs, transaction IDs, hashes, URLs, or
  timestamps inside an image model.
- Render all exact text as deterministic HTML or SVG after image generation.
- Keep headings short and labels readable after video compression.
- Maintain a caption-safe lower margin of at least 18% of the canvas.

Branding and provenance:
- Overlay the real DeltaSignal logo in post-processing.
- Add “DeltaSignal logo and visual identity © 2026 DeltaSignal.”
- Embed truthful StrategiX provenance metadata.
- Produce one sidecar receipt per final infographic with prompt and output
  SHA-256 hashes.

Common negative prompt:
No invented text, fake IDs, fake HashScan pages, fake Hedera branding, private
keys, seed phrases, wallet secrets, QR codes, humanoid robots, stock charts,
Bitcoin symbols, flying coins, speculative-price imagery, excessive neon,
cyberpunk clutter, illegible microtext, or misleading live-payment animation.

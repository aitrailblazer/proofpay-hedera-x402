PURPOSE

These prompts create one supporting visualization for each of the eleven demo
slides. The visualization is the cinematic evidence layer behind or beside the
deterministic slide text; it does not replace the slide, narration, captions, or
proof data.

Use a 16:9 landscape canvas at 1920 × 1080 or higher. Generate at high quality.
Exact account IDs, transaction IDs, hashes, timestamps, receipt IDs, commands,
and captions must remain deterministic HTML/SVG overlays. Do not ask an image
model to reproduce those strings.

GLOBAL VISUAL SYSTEM — INCLUDE WITH EVERY PROMPT

Create a premium cinematic technology visualization for DeltaSignal ProofPay,
designed as a supporting layer for a professional Hedera x402 bounty
presentation. 16:9 landscape, dark near-black background, deep graphite and
subtle midnight-green surfaces, restrained Hedera green and DeltaSignal red
accents, warm gold reserved for verified payment evidence, clean geometric
lighting, crisp edges, high contrast, sophisticated financial-infrastructure
editorial style, subtle volumetric depth, generous negative space, no people,
no coins flying through the air, no speculative trading imagery, no price
charts, no cyberpunk clutter. Keep the center-left and lower safe areas clear
when requested for deterministic slide text. Use abstract symbols and spatial
relationships instead of generated paragraphs. The visualization must feel
credible, audit-grade, and technically precise rather than promotional.

BRANDING AND PROVENANCE

- Use the real DeltaSignal logo as a deterministic post-processing overlay,
  not an AI-invented logo.
- Place the logo in the top-right or bottom-right safe area without covering
  evidence.
- Add deterministic visible text:
  “DeltaSignal logo and visual identity © 2026 DeltaSignal.”
- Embed truthful StrategiX image provenance metadata and create a signed
  sidecar receipt for every final image.
- Default final format: PNG.
- Suggested output names:
  slide-01-opening.png through slide-11-thank-you.png.
